# Lesson Robot Privacy Policy

_Last updated: April 24, 2023_

## Overview

Lesson Robot is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information.

### Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@lessonrobot.com.

### Collection of Personal Information

We collect personal information, such as name, email, and IP addresses when you sign up for an account or use our service. We use consent as our legal basis for collecting and processing personal information.

### Use of Personal Information

We use personal information to allow individuals to sign up for accounts, send marketing emails, and use our service. If you do not provide the information we request, you will not be able to create an account, receive emails or use the service.

### Disclosure of Personal Information

We may share personal information with marketing providers, payment processors, hosting, and analytics tools. We will not disclose your personal information to any third party without your consent, except as required by law.

### Rights of EU Residents

EU residents have the right to request access, portability, rectification, deletion, object to processing, and complain to relevant authorities. If you are an EU resident and would like to exercise your privacy rights, please contact us at support@lessonrobot.com.

### Storage of Personal Information

We store personal information for a period of three years.

### Transfer of Personal Information

Personal information may be transferred to the United States and Canada. By using our service, you consent to the transfer of your personal information to these countries.

### Cookies

We use cookies for authentication purposes.

### Notification of Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify individuals of changes by updating this page or sending an email.

## Conclusion

By using our service, you agree to the terms of this privacy policy. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us at support@lessonrobot.com.
